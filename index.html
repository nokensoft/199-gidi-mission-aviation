Background

Why Do We Need This Aircraft?

The GIDI ministry field spans across the highest mountain peaks and deepest valleys, entirely unreachable by land transportation.

!

Current Ministry Challenges

For years, essential services like baptisms, deploying teachers, and emergency medical aid have been heavily hindered by highly expensive and limited commercial transportation. Securing an independent fleet is an absolute necessity for the sustainability of the mission.

Purpose of Procurement

4 Pillars of Aviation Ministry

Supporting the ministry of the GIDI Church in bringing the Gospel, medicine, and hope to unreachable regions, becoming a blessing to all nations.

Evangelism

Sending missionaries to unreached areas, both domestically and internationally.

Church Ministry

Facilitating coordination and pastoral visits across GIDI regions scattered throughout the interior.

Humanitarian Mission

Emergency medical evacuation (medivac) for congregations and local communities in critical conditions across remote Papua.

Logistics Distribution

Delivering Bibles, school textbooks, medical supplies, and basic necessities for serving ministers.
